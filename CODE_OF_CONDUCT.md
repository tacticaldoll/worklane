# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want `worklane` to be a focused, respectful project where people can discuss
job semantics, reliability tradeoffs, and implementation details without being
talked down to or pushed away.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about risks and tradeoffs.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on behavior, evidence, and maintenance
  cost.
- Welcome contributors who are learning the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption after maintainers ask for a change in behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, block accounts, or take
other reasonable actions to keep the project healthy.

To report a conduct issue, open a minimal issue asking for a maintainer contact
path without sharing sensitive details publicly. If the issue is also a security
concern, follow [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md).
